Scholarships

Dixie Technical College offers many types of scholarships to students based on specific criteria. To apply for a Dixie Tech scholarship, download the application. If you need assistance or for more information, contact Student Services at 674-8400 to schedule an appointment and complete the application process.


Download Dixie Tech Scholarship Application here


Dixie Tech Merit Based Scholarship – The Dixie Technical College Merit Based Scholarships may be awarded to students based on: their previous educational accomplishments, grades, competency level, participation in student organizations, community service, work experience, etc. Full or Part tuition waivers are available on a limited number basis.


Dixie Tech Needs Based Scholarship – the Dixie Technical College Needs Based Scholarships may be awarded to students who are in good academic standing who show a financial need. Full or part-tuition waivers are available on a limited number basis. Students must apply for Federal Financial Aid prior to submitting the Dixie Tech Needs Based Scholarship application. For questions on applying for Federal Financial Aid, contact Kami Alldredge at 435-674-8415.


Dixie Tech Single Parent Scholarship – The Dixie Technical College Single Parent Scholarship may be awarded to students who are single parents of minor children. Students must apply for Federal Financial Aid prior to submitting the Dixie Tech Single Parent Scholarship application. For questions on applying for Federal Financial Aid, contact Kami Alldredge at 435-674-8415.


Dixie Tech Bridge Scholarship – The Dixie Technical College offers a Bridge Scholarship to students graduating from high school and previously enrolled in a certificate program. Tuition waivers are available for the remainder of the summer term (end of May to the end of August) on a limited basis.Students must apply for Federal Financial Aid prior to submitting the Dixie Tech Bridge Scholarship application. For questions on applying for Federal Financial Aid, contact Kami Alldredge at 435-674-8415.


Dixie Elks Scholarship – AThe Dixie Elks Scholarship sponsors current Dixie Tech students in their final or next to final course. Students applying for the Elks Scholarship must have and maintain satisfactory academic progress. For more information, contact Kami Alldredge at (435) 674-8415.


NASA Scholarship – Available to students interested in and enrolled in programs, occupations, and technologies that are supportive of or related to the aerospace industry. For more information contact Kami Alldredge at 435-674-8415.


SUTA Scholarship – The Southern Utah Trucking Association sponsors scholarships for students entering the Diesel Technology or CDL programs. Interested students must fill out the application and submit it by the spring or fall deadline. For more information, contact Kami Alldredge at (435) 674-8415. (Download application here)


SUHBA Scholarship – The Southern Utah Home Builders Association offers scholarships to students intending to be employed in the Construction Industry. Preference for scholarships will be given to students pursuing construction-related education and students affiliated with SUHBA. The scholarship may be used to fund almost any post-secondary education, i.e., college or vocational, public or private. Recipients may be awarded up to $1,000 per academic year.

Applicant is responsible to ensure that all application materials and requirements are submitted to The Southern Utah Home Builders Association by March 31, 2018. Application must be complete or it will not be considered. Contact Southern Utah Homebuilders at (435) 674-1400 or visit their office at 2303 N Coral Canyon Boulevard, Suite 200, Washington, UT. Visit them online at suhba.com. (Download application here)


UHEAA – Dixie Tech offers a need-based scholarship program for currently-enrolled students. It is sponsored by the Utah Higher Education Assistance Authority. The number of scholarships awarded will depend on the number of qualified applicants and the amount of scholarship money available. For information, contact Kami Alldredge at (435) 674-8415.
