Custom Fit


The Utah Legislature allocates Custom Fit funds each year to encourage companies to pursue training that will maintain and grow Utah’s businesses and economy. What does Custom Fit have to offer your company?


  • Funding: Up to 40% of training costs may be paid for your company
  • Customer Service: A designated training representative is available to help determine your company’s training needs
  • Customization: Training is customized to meet the needs of your organization
  • Ease: Training programs can be established quickly
  • Flexibility: Training can be held at Dixie Technical College, at your company, or at another training facility depending on your needs
  • Quality: State-of-the-art training and development is provided by world-class instructors

Our goal is to assist your company in building and maintaining a superior workforce to strengthen your organization’s competitive edge. Qualifying is easy! If your company is for-profit and located in Washington County, schedule an appointment today.
