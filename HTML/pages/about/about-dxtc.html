About Dixie Tech

Located in the Tonaquint area of sunny St. George, the Dixie Technical College is one of eight regional colleges that comprise the Utah System of Technical Colleges. DixieTech is the premier college for skill and competency based training in Washington County.


DixieTech was created during a special session of the Utah State Legislature in 2001 through House Bill 1003 which created the Utah system of Technical Colleges and its regional campuses: Bridgerland, Davis, Dixie, Mountainland, Ogden-Weber, Tooele, Southwest , and Uintah Basin. The USTC Board of Trustees provides oversight and governs the activities of the eight campuses.


Our charge is to provide relevant skill and competency based training to prepare students for good jobs. To that end, the DixieTech commits to our vision mission, and guiding principles:


Forward Thinking, Future Focused, Career Ready
