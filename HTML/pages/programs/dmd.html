Digital Media Design

The Digital Media Design program is an opportunity to elevate student learning and prepare a new generation for exciting careers in the rapidly growing industry of Digital Media. Students will learn the programs, tools and languages needed to be successful and marketable in todays design and web development market. Industry partners drive curriculum and take an active role in the classroom.


Students will gain knowledge of the following:

  • Adobe Creative Suite
  • Photography and Videography
  • Social Media and Marketing Essentials
  • 3d Modeling
  • Web and App Development
