COLLISION REPAIR

The Collision Repair Program at Dixie Tech is aligned with the Inter-Industry Conference on Auto Collision Repair (I-CAR). Upon successful completion of the program, students will be eligible for I-CAR ProLevel testing through I-CAR's Professional Development Program. The collision repair courses will provide instruction in the fundamentals of repairing vehicles damaged in a collision.


Students will gain knowledge of the following:

  • Aluminum panel reapir
  • replacement and alignment
  • glass replacement and alignment
  • MIG and steel welding
  • structural measuring and straightening
  • Heating and cooling systems
  • electrical circuits
  • Refinishing
