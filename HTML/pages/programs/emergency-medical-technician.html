Emergency Medical Technician

Training in this program will prepare students for the administration of specialized emergency care and the transportation of victims of acute illness or injury to a medical facility. Students will learn roles necessary to work as part of an EMS team. Upon completion of all course requirements, students will be recommended for written and practical NREMT testing for EMT certification.


Students will gain knowledge in the following:

  • Patient Assessments
  • Vital Signs
  • Manage Airways using Airway Adjuncts
  • Bandage and Splint
  • Spine Immobilization
  • Patient Assisted Medication Administration
  • Assist in Emergency Childbirth
  • Treat and Transport Patients Safely

Course Pre-Requisites:

  • Current immunization records
  • Current TB Testing (Within 1 year)
  • Current CPR Health Care Provider Card
  • Must be 18 years of age within 120 days of course completion
  • Blood Pressure Cuff and Stethoscope
