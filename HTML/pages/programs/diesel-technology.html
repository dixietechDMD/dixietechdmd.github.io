Diesel

The Diesel Technology program at Dixie Technical College provides students with an opportunity to increase their diesel mechanics skills in order to pursue a career as a diesel technician. Career path courses are aligned with the National Automotive Technicians Education Foundation (NATEF) standards.


Students will gain knowledge of the following:

  • Heavy Duty Diesel Electrical & HVAC
  • Heavy Duty Diesel Power Trains
  • Heavy Duty Diesel Chassis
  • Preventative Maintenance
  • Heavy Duty Brakes
  • Steering and Suspension
  • Intro to CDL & Welding
  • Hydraulics
  • Drive Trains
  • Engines
  • Engine Management Systems
  • Heavy Duty HVAC
  • Electrical Systems
