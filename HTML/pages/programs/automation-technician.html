Industrial Automation Technician

One of the job skills gaps in America is the lack of employees trained & certified in Industrial & Facilities Maintenance, yet these jobs are good paying and the career path is strong. This program is designed to prepare students for highly technical maintenance jobs through a combination of classroom and hands on lab workshop experiences coupled with practical assignments back on the job. Training focuses on high demand skill sets, building from basic concepts through advanced applications.


Students will gain knowledge of the following:

  • Broad-spectrum Technician Proficiencies
  • Mechanical Drive Systems
  • Pneumatics & Hydraulics
  • Electrical & Electronic Motor Control Systems
  • Programmable Logic Controllers
  • Robotics & Factory Automation
  • Leadership & Facility Asset Management
