Medical Assisting

Medical assistants work alongside the physician, typically in an outpatient facility. They are trained to perform both administrative and clinical duties. Students will complete a 180 hour unpaid externship in a clinical setting.


Students will gain knowledge of the following:

  • Understand the use of medical terminology in a medical office environment
  • Perform medical front office tasks including scheduling and patient flow, maintaining medical records, and basic insurance billing and coding
  • Provide clinical office patient care including vital signs, medical histories, preparing patients for examination, laboratory testing and phlebotomy
  • Preparation for National exams in Phlebotomy and EKG technician.
