Phlebotomy Technician

The Phlebotomy Program provides students with understanding of body systems, how to conduct lab tests, collect specimens, and perform venipunctures.


Students will gain knowledge in the following:

  • Define the associated medical terms
  • Describe basic human anatomy and physiology
  • Trace the circulatory pathway of the blood
  • Identify anatomical structures utilized in blood collection
  • Describe the components of blood
  • Perform the blood collection procedure
  • Participate in performance improvement/quality assurance
  • Demonstrate skills required for pre-analytical specimen processing
  • Discuss specimen collection techniques requiring special handling
  • Perform clerical duties required to document phlebotomy practice
